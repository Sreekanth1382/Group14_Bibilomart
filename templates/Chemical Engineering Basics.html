Chemical Engineering Basics

Author: James Riggs

"Chemical Engineering Basics" by James Riggs is a foundational textbook offering a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental principles of chemical engineering. Authored by James H. Riggs, this book serves as an essential resource for students embarking on their journey into the field of chemical engineering.

Covering key topics such as material balances, energy balances, fluid flow, heat transfer, and chemical reactions, "Chemical Engineering Basics" provides clear explanations and practical examples to aid comprehension. Riggs aims to equip readers with the knowledge and skills necessary to solve engineering problems and understand core concepts in chemical processes.

Michael Johnson

"While 'Chemical Engineering Basics' covers all the essential topics, I found some sections to be lacking in depth. The explanations are clear, but I wished for more advanced discussions and real-world examples. Additionally, the exercises at the end of chapters could be more challenging to fully test comprehension. Overall, it's a good starting point for beginners, but readers may need to supplement it with additional resources for a deeper understanding of certain topics."
