The Art of Photography

Author: Jeff Raskin

"The Art of Photography" by Jeff Raskin is a comprehensive guidebook that explores the principles, techniques, and aesthetics of photography. Authored by Jeff Raskin, an experienced photographer and educator, this book offers invaluable insights for photographers of all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Covering a wide range of topics such as composition, lighting, exposure, color theory, and post-processing, "The Art of Photography" provides practical advice, creative inspiration, and technical know-how to help readers improve their photography skills and develop their artistic vision.

Michael Thompson

"'The Art of Photography' offers a wealth of information and advice, I found some sections to be overly basic, particularly for more experienced photographers. The book covers essential topics comprehensively, but I wished for more advanced techniques and insights to challenge and inspire seasoned practitioners. Additionally, the emphasis on digital photography may not fully cater to photographers who prefer analog or alternative processes. Overall, it's a great resource for beginners and enthusiasts, but more experienced photographers may find it lacking in depth."
