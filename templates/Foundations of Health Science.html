Foundations of Health Science

Author: Barbara Osborn Henkel

"Foundations of Health Science" serves as a comprehensive guide to various topics within the realm of health science. Authored by a team of experts in the field, this textbook provides a thorough exploration of foundational principles, theories, and practices essential for understanding health-related disciplines. Covering a broad spectrum of subjects, ranging from anatomy and physiology to epidemiology and healthcare systems, the book offers a holistic approach to health science education. Through its integration of theoretical knowledge and practical applications, "Foundations of Health Science" equips students and professionals alike with the necessary tools to navigate the complexities of the healthcare landscape.
