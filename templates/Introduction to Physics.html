Introduction to Physics

Author: Jim Breithaupt

"Introduction to Physics" by Jim Breithaupt is a widely used textbook that offers a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental principles of physics. Designed for students at the high school or introductory college level, the book covers topics ranging from mechanics and thermodynamics to electromagnetism and modern physics.

With clear explanations and numerous examples, Breithaupt aims to demystify physics concepts and make them accessible to students with varying levels of prior knowledge. The book typically includes a variety of exercises and problems to reinforce understanding and encourage critical thinking.
