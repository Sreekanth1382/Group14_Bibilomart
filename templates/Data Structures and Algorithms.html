Data Structures and Algorithms

Author: Prof Hawkins

"Data Structures and Algorithms" by Prof. Hawkins is a comprehensive textbook that delves into the fundamental concepts of organizing and processing data efficiently. Authored by Professor Hawkins, this book is designed for students and professionals in computer science and related fields.

Covering essential topics such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, sorting algorithms, and searching algorithms, "Data Structures and Algorithms" provides clear explanations, pseudocode, and implementation examples to aid comprehension. The book aims to equip readers with the knowledge and skills needed to design and analyze algorithms and data structures for solving real-world problems efficiently.
