Advanced Topics in Health Informatics

Author: Professor Richard M. Greenes

"Advanced Topics in Health Informatics" authored by Professor Richard M. Greenes is an authoritative exploration of complex subjects within the field of health informatics. As a distinguished expert in the field, Professor Greenes delves into advanced concepts and emerging trends that are shaping the future of healthcare technology and information management.

This comprehensive textbook covers a wide array of topics such as health data analytics, clinical decision support systems, telemedicine, artificial intelligence in healthcare, and privacy and security issues in health information technology. Through detailed explanations, case studies, and practical examples, Professor Greenes provides readers with an in-depth understanding of the latest advancements and challenges in health informatics.
