Computer Science Fundamentals

Author: Alan Johnson

"Computer Science Fundamentals" by Alan Johnson is a comprehensive guide aimed at providing a solid foundation in computer science principles. It covers essential topics such as algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and computational thinking. Through clear explanations and practical examples, Johnson aims to equip readers with the fundamental knowledge needed to pursue further studies or careers in computer science.

Daniel Ramirez

"While 'Computer Science Fundamentals' covers a wide range of topics, I found some sections lacking in depth. The explanations are clear, but I wished for more advanced discussions and real-world applications. Additionally, the exercises at the end of chapters could be more challenging to test understanding thoroughly. Overall, it's a good starting point, but readers may need supplemental resources for a deeper dive into certain areas."
